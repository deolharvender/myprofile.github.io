About us

Established in June 2016, Sankalp Welfare Society was pledged to help human beings.

It is a Non Governmental Organization, working for human health and to empower women and children’s in society in all over U.P.

Sankalp Welfare Society formed by few members in 2016, which includes different health service persons and local bodies.

Our Goals

  1. To empower every women
  2. To make every children sound with technical education.
  3. To provide self defense training every female.
  4. To make availability of proper health services in every village.
  5. To create awareness among the public related to health and education.

Our Plans

We want to make every woman self depended so that she can earn respect for herself in the society. Our NGO want to train every woman with self defense training so that she can protect herself.

Our aim to equipped children’s with technical education so that they can live their life with respect in society.

Our objective to provide health services in villages, where well educated doctors are not available so that they can get basic treatment of disease without any delay.

We want to make our society related to health and importance of education of every child and woman.
